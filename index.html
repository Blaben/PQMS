Welcome

to the all new UEW Past Question Management System

Want to learn how things works?

Never get Confused. Make sure to explore all the steps outlined here to have access to your desired past question.

Step 1

Students : Login with your index number as your username and applicant id as your password

Lecturer : Login with your staff credentials to gain access to the past question management system

Step 2

After Successful login credentials, you will be directed to the espective dashboard.

Use the Dashboard thus the sidebar view to browse for past questions. From Level of student to the semester to the department and then to the question

Step 3

As a Lecturer you have access to upload and view past questions from the past question management system

As a student, you only have access to view and download the past questions. NB: you cannot upload a past question to the system
